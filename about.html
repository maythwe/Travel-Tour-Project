ABOUT US

The Company

May Travel and Tour started its business in 1994 just two years before the official visit Myanmar year in 1996 and licensed by the Ministry of Hotel & Tourism (Kha/0184). May Travel and Tour is assisted by highly skilled people and is a well-recognized destination management company focusing on customer service with Care, Empathy and Hospitality.
With its registered head office in Yangon, branch office in Mandalay and Regional directors in Bagan and Inle Lake, May Travel and Tour is a well-recognized by the international tourists and industry partners. We have our experienced staff members at Yangon head office working towards the same goal of customer satisfaction! Our star guides take excellent care on customers and provide rich background information to enhance traveler’s experiences.
